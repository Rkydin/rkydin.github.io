## Project Overview

Mechanical Design Intern supporting Volta's laser-based wireless power beaming hardware — CAD, optical fixture design, and hands-on electromechanical build and test support, including a shipping enclosure for a laser assembly delivered to the European Space Agency (ESA).

## Responsibilities

**CAD & Mechanical Design:**
* Designed a CAD enclosure housing a laser assembly and its associated optical fixtures for shipment and testing, supporting delivery to the European Space Agency (ESA)
* Developed SolidWorks CAD models and mechanical hardware for internal and electro-optical support applications
* Designed optical-mount fixtures and supported hardware development for future thermal vacuum (TVAC) testing

**Documentation & Build Support:**
* Maintained drawing and BOM revision control through ECOs and redlines, ensuring documentation accuracy across engineering and technical reporting
* Gained hands-on exposure to electromechanical assembly, including soldering and cable harnessing, through hardware build support

## Skills Applied

* SolidWorks (parts, assemblies, drawings)
* Optical fixture and mount design
* Thermal vacuum (TVAC) test support
* ECO / redline documentation control
* Electromechanical assembly (soldering, cable harnessing)
* Cross-functional engineering collaboration
